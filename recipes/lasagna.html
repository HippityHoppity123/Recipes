Lasagna

Description

Delectable lasagna with layers of pasta sauce, meats, onions, garlic, lasagna noodles, and various types of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sauce
  • Cheese (The more types the better)
  • Meat (sausage, beef, etc.)
  • Spices
  • Pasta
  • Onions
  • Garlics
  1. Boil pasta: Boil pasta in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prep meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixtureStir cheese mixture in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce, and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
  5. Season: Season lasagna with spices.
